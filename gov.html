Who this is for

Working in education, arts or community?

Blue $nake Studio is an independent creative practice open to conversations, pilots and collaborations with the following groups.

Schools and teachers

Classroom teachers, education support officers, integration aides and school leaders looking for creative, child-safe learning resources and workshop support.

Council arts / youth / community teams

Local government arts officers, community development staff, youth workers and recreation coordinators interested in printable packs, QR trails, creative workshops or local partnerships.

Disability arts organisations

Arts organisations, support coordinators and community groups seeking creative identity tools, visual art activities and symbolic learning material that respects diverse learners.

Libraries and learning spaces

Public libraries, maker spaces and community learning centres interested in printable resources, interactive browser tools or pop-up creative sessions.

Family-friendly creative programs

Organisations running family events, holiday programs, after-school activities or early childhood creative sessions looking for print-and-play activities.

Local festivals / public art / workshops

Festival coordinators, public art programs, community theatre and event organisers interested in QR poster trails, visual identity work or participatory creative workshops.

What B$S can offer

Creative tools and project ideas.

Current capabilities and areas where Blue $nake Studio can work with community partners. All subject to conversation and realistic scoping.

Printable classroom resources

Colouring sheets, lyric pages, glyph worksheets, QR drop sheets and symbolic learning packs. Print-and-use, no login required.

Child-safe creative technology concepts

Meta-Pet: a browser-first, privacy-first digital learning companion concept. No ads, no trackers, no student data collection. School pilot conversations welcome.

Music / lyric / language learning sheets

Chant sheets, lyric posters and bilingual learning sheets (including Frankston → Fuji Japanese–English materials) for classroom or community use.

QR poster and street-print trails

Print-ready A4 QR posters and sticker sheets linking physical spaces back to digital content. Officeworks-compatible, seven colour variants available.

Visual art and symbolic identity packs

Symbolic identity systems, studio artwork packs and mythic visual world assets for creative learning, display and community celebration.

Creative workshops

School sessions, disability arts workshops, print-your-own-symbol activities, music and rhythm workshops, and worldbuilding creative activities. Contact to discuss scope and availability.

Disability-aware learning support material

Visual and symbolic learning tools designed with calm, structured, non-punitive frameworks in mind. Not clinical tools — creative support for diverse learners.

Youth-friendly worldbuilding activities

Creative identity design, character building, symbol making and mythology exploration for young people in school, youth or community settings.

What Blue $nake Studio is not claiming to be

  • Not a government service — Blue $nake Studio is an independent creative practice based in Frankston, not a government-funded or government-endorsed body.
  • Not a school-approved program — current tools have not been formally assessed or endorsed by any school, education department or curriculum authority.
  • Not an NDIS provider — Blue $nake Studio does not hold NDIS provider registration and does not deliver NDIS-funded services.
  • Not a clinical therapy service — creative workshops and learning tools are not a substitute for clinical, psychological, medical or legal advice.
  • Not a cultural authority — research into Bunurong–Boonwurrung language (Waahn) is framed as respectful in-progress research, not an approved translation or cultural authority.
  • Not a replacement for qualified advice — none of the studio's outputs replace qualified education, health, disability, legal or community services.
