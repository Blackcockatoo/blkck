Street Print Pack

A practical checklist for turning blkck2 / B$S into physical artefacts: A4 posters, hand cards, QR stickers, coasters, lyric sheets, zine pages and local print drops.

B$S

Purpose

Street print is the bridge between the screen and the hand. Every piece should do three things fast: look strange enough to stop someone, say what it is, and give one clean scan path back to the portal.

A4 poster recipe

  • One big image or mark.
  • One hook line.
  • One QR code only.
  • One short URL: blkck2.com
  • One B$S mark.
  • Leave white cut margin.

Card / sticker recipe

  • Front: symbol + hook.
  • Back: QR + one sentence.
  • No tiny clutter.
  • High contrast.
  • Test scan before printing.
  • Use matte if readability matters.

QR rules

  • Do not squash the code.
  • Keep clear space around it.
  • Use dark code on light field when possible.
  • Test on two phones.
  • Print one test sheet first.

Print note

Officeworks/local printer ready means simple A4 ratios, readable type, no micro-text, enough margin for trimming, and scan-tested QR codes. Start black/white cheap, then upgrade colour once the design proves it works.
