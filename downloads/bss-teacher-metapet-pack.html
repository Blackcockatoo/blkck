Clean description

Meta-Pet is a privacy-first digital learning companion concept from Blue $nake Studio. Teacher Tools is the printable branch: transition sheets, support templates, attention supports and practical classroom prompts.

Design posture

  • Simple printable formats.
  • Low-admin setup.
  • Student-friendly language.
  • Transparent purpose.
  • Printable first, digital second.

Who it helps

  • Classroom teachers.
  • Education support staff.
  • Integration aides.
  • Parents needing simple routines.
  • Creative learners who think in symbols, colours and patterns.

Use cases

  • Morning check-in sheet.
  • Transition support flow.
  • Calm/focus/power colour mapping.
  • Pet-symbol identity exercise.
  • Behaviour as communication prompt.

Boundary

This is not legal, medical or clinical advice. It is a practical creative support pack for learning, communication and classroom routines. Schools should adapt it to their own policies and professional judgement.

Thirty-second pitch

Blue $nake Studio is building simple creative tools that give teachers more printable support without adding admin. Meta-Pet turns attention, emotion, routine and pattern recognition into a friendly symbolic companion. Teacher Tools turns that into real-world sheets that can be printed, trialled and adapted.

Starter activity

  1. Name a Meta-Pet that helps with one learning problem.
  2. Choose three traits: calm, focus, courage, patience, kindness, reset.
  3. Pick three colours: calm colour, focus colour, power colour.
  4. Draw the pet symbol.
  5. Write one sentence: “When I feel stuck, my Meta-Pet reminds me to…”
