# B$S Gallery Rooms Manifest

The gallery must feel like rooms in a myth-tech studio, not categories in a portfolio.

## Room law
Each room needs five things:

1. **Doorway line** — a short phrase that feels like entering a chamber.
2. **Mood** — the sensory feel of the room.
3. **Use** — what this room is practically for.
4. **Proof** — what assets already prove it exists.
5. **Next action** — what the visitor should click, scan, download, or remember.

## Rooms

### Room 01 — The Painted Wing
Handmade paintings, Auralia, cockatoos, halos, brush texture and the artist's real hand.

### Room 02 — Neon Venom
QR portals, sticker bait, music promo, @blkck2 traffic and Black Wing Crew hooks.

### Room 03 — The Omen Room
Black birds, black butterflies, chant sheets, lyric-world material and recursive omen language.

### Room 04 — The Learning Beast
Meta-Pet, school pilot, teacher tools, classroom proof and behaviour-support direction.

### Room 05 — Moss 60 Engine
Symbolic geometry, 108/360, base-60 logic, digital DNA and hidden game mechanics.

### Room 06 — Street Print Arsenal
Posters, QR cards, sticker sheets, cutouts and print-ready physical promo assets.

## Design rule
Every room must look like a spell but work like a tool.
