## Page purpose

### Home
A high-impact overview page. It introduces Blue Snake Studios, shows the strongest feature image, and points people into the main collections.

### Auralia
The mythic character-world page. Use it for Auralia, the cockatoo, serpent, gold halo, dream-sign imagery, feminine archetype and symbolic listening system.

### Insidious Rhythms
The lyric poster page. Use it for recursive poem posters, spoken-word visuals, rhythm/memetics writing and hidden cadence imagery.

### Black Wing Crew
The chant and dark music mythology page. Use it for Black Omen, Black Wing Chant, black birds, black butterflies and rowing-chant lyric sheets.

### Neon Venom
The music-world promotion page. Use it for QR stickers, neon merch graphics, album visuals and promotional art.

### Paintings
The handmade work page. Use it for canvas pieces, hand-drawn boards, photographed paintings, sketches and physical art.

### B$S Branding
The studio identity page. Use it for logos, marks, emblems, cockatoo icons, typography and brand systems.

### MOSS60 / Geometry
The symbolic systems page. Use it for diagrams, sigils, linework, sacred geometry, modular patterns and experimental visual mathematics.

### Process / Sketchbook
The behind-the-scenes page. Use it for references, bird studies, source photos, mood boards, work-in-progress material and raw concepts.

### About
The studio statement. Explain the connection between art, lyric writing, memetics, behaviour-language thinking, music-world branding and symbolic systems.

### Contact
Enquiries, collaboration, commissions, cultural projects, prints, stickers and web/gallery work.
