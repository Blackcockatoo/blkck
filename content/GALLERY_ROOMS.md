# B$S Gallery Rooms

The gallery should avoid boring folder labels. It should feel like walking through the Blue $nake Studio archive.

## The Painted Wing

Handmade paintings, Auralia portraits, cockatoo companions, mirror figures and spiral-listener imagery.

Purpose: show the actual hand, not just the digital output.

## Neon Venom

QR portals, stickers, black birds, venom colours, scan-me art and music promo energy.

Purpose: pull people toward YouTube / Black Wing Crew / street-promo drops.

## The Omen Room

Black Wing Crew, Black Omen, Insidious Rhythms, black birds, black butterflies and recursive lyric language.

Purpose: hold the darker ceremonial song-world.

## The Learning Beast

Meta-Pet, Meta-Pet Pilot School, teacher tools, healthy attention design, classroom proof and behavioural support ideas.

Purpose: show the practical education engine under the myth, including the live pilot-school link: https://metapet-pilot-school.vercel.app/.

## Moss 60 Engine

Number theory, 108 / 360 geometry, symbolic maths, glyph logic and digital DNA direction.

Purpose: keep the hidden engine visible enough to be credible without over-explaining it.

## Street Print Arsenal

Posters, stickers, QR sheets, lyric sheets, cutouts, promo cards and Officeworks-ready physical assets.

Purpose: make B$S leave the screen.
