How to use them

Print. Post. Scan.

  1. Download the pack Get the QR Drop Pack — seven print-ready PNG files, one per colour. High resolution, black background, designed for printing.
  2. Choose your size Each QR scans reliably at A6 (postcard), A5 (half-A4) or A4. Do not print smaller than 5cm × 5cm or scanning accuracy drops. A6 is the sweet spot for stickers and handouts.
  3. Print at Officeworks or home Laser print on matte or gloss paper for outdoor use. Inkjet works indoors. For sticker sheets: print on sticker paper (A4 self-adhesive matte, available at Officeworks and newsagents).
  4. Laminate if outdoor Laminated QR codes last months on brick, metal and wood surfaces. A3 thermal laminator sheets from Officeworks, ~$15 for a 100-sheet pack. Trim with scissors after laminating.
  5. Post and drop Lamp posts, windows, noticeboards, guitar cases, sketchbooks, cafe counter, venue door, school whiteboard. Ask permission where needed. The code scans through glass.
  6. Test the scan Before your run, scan the printed code with your phone camera. Confirm it opens blackwingcrew.netlify.app. If the image is blurry or the code is too small, reprint at a larger size.

Where they work

Campaign ideas.

Music venue drops

Sticker a set of 6 colours on the venue door, backstage, bar or merch table. Colour variety = visual intrigue. One scan = streaming.

School music rooms

Laminated A5 QR card on the music room wall or noticeboard. Students scan during breaks. Works without login or download.

Street poster campaigns

Paste alongside a lyric poster or Neon Venom print. The poster gives context; the QR gives the destination. Works as a pair.

Handouts at events

A6 card with QR on one side, "Black Wing Crew / Neon Venom LP" on the other. Hand out at markets, open mics, art shows, corridors.

Zine and print inserts

Insert a small QR card into a zine, print pack or school resource. Low cost, turns physical into digital.

Teacher resource packs

Add a QR code to the Teacher Tools pack pointing to Meta-Pet. Print it alongside the cheatsheet templates.
