# Blue Snake Studio
## Profile — Frankston 2035

---

### Who We Are

Blue Snake Studio is a Melbourne-based independent creative studio working at the intersection of visual art, music, technology, and civic life.

We are not a consultancy. We are not an agency. We are a creative practice that builds things.

---

### What We Do

We design creative systems — not campaigns.

Our work includes public art, music production, educational tools, interactive experiences, and civic design. We specialise in projects where art and purpose are inseparable.

---

### Our Role in This Project

Blue Snake Studio is the engine of Frankston 2035. We provide:

- Creative direction and quality control across all outputs
- Design and production of artworks, tools, and educational materials
- Project management and community facilitation
- Partnership brokerage (artists, schools, libraries, cultural organisations)
- Documentation and knowledge transfer

We are not the face of the project. Frankston is.

---

### What We Bring

| Capability | Relevance to Frankston 2035 |
|---|---|
| Public art design and production | Mural programs, permanent installations |
| Educational content design | Curriculum-linked artwork, teacher resources |
| Healthy technology design | Screen-safe tools, offline-first experiences |
| Community engagement | Workshops, participation programs, co-design |
| Music and audio production | Sound archives, community recordings, compositions |
| Cultural protocol experience | Navigating complex multi-stakeholder environments |
| Documentation systems | Registers, playbooks, handoff materials |

---

### How We Work

We work in long cycles, not quick deliveries.

A mural is not done when the paint dries. It is done when the school has a lesson plan, the community knows the story, and the next generation knows who made it and why.

We document everything. We transfer everything. We do not create dependency.

---

### What We Do Not Do

- We do not speak for First Nations communities.
- We do not manage funds on behalf of other organisations.
- We do not make cultural claims outside our own practice.
- We do not accept projects that require surveillance of children.
- We do not chase scale at the cost of quality.
