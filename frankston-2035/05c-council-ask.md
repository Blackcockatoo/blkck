# Council Ask
## Frankston 2035 — What We Are Requesting from Frankston City Council

---

### The Short Version

Partner with us. Not as a funder of last resort — as a first-mover civic leader who sees what Frankston can become.

---

### What We Are Asking For

**1. Partnership Status**
A formal written partnership agreement acknowledging Frankston 2035 as a Council-supported creative initiative. This does not require Council to fund everything. It requires Council to name the project as a priority.

**2. Seed Funding — Pilots**
A co-investment contribution toward the three pilot projects. Suggested Council contribution: $60,000–$80,000 across the pilot phase (2026–2027), matched by grant funding sought independently by Blue Snake Studio.

**3. Permit Facilitation**
Streamlined processing for public art permits, site access agreements, and temporary installation approvals. Not a guarantee of approval — a commitment to active facilitation.

**4. Venue Access**
Priority access to Council-managed venues for community engagement sessions, workshops, and public presentations related to the project.

**5. A Healthy Technology Statement**
A Council resolution affirming that child-facing Council programs will be evaluated against the Healthy Technology Charter. No mandate — an alignment statement.

---

### What We Are Not Asking For

- Full project funding
- Staff time beyond a single designated liaison
- Brand co-ownership or naming rights
- Exclusivity over Frankston's creative programming
- Approval of creative decisions

---

### What Council Receives

| Council contributes | Council receives |
|---|---|
| Partnership endorsement | First-mover credibility in civic creativity nationally |
| Seed co-investment ($60–80k) | Matched funding leveraged by BSS (target 3:1) |
| Permit facilitation | Three permanent public assets in the city |
| Venue access | A replicable model named after Frankston |
| Healthy Technology Statement | Alignment with national children's digital safety agenda |

---

### The Ask in One Sentence

We are not asking Council to run this project. We are asking Council to believe in it, open the doors, and share in the outcome.

---

### Proposed Next Step

A 45-minute briefing with the relevant Council officer and one Councillor.
No slide deck. A conversation.
