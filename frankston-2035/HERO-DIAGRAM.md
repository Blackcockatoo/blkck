## SVG Specification

### Node 1 — FRANKSTON (top)
- Shape: Full-width horizontal band, 30mm tall
- Fill: Frankston Blue (`#1B4F8A`)
- Text: "FRANKSTON" — 28pt Canela Light, white, centred
- Subtext: "A city that invests in creativity" — 10pt Neue Haas Grotesk, white, centred

### Row 2 — Infrastructure (three nodes)
- Shapes: Three equal rounded rectangles, 55mm wide × 18mm tall
- Fill: Frankston Blue at 20% opacity
- Stroke: 1pt Frankston Blue
- Labels: "PUBLIC ART" / "SCHOOLS" / "LIBRARIES" — 9pt Plex Mono, uppercase, Deep Ink, centred

### Connector label between Row 2 and Row 3

- Position: centred, between rows

### Primary Flow Arrows

## What the Diagram Answers

| Question | Answer visible in diagram |
|---|---|
| What is this project? | A cycle: invest in infrastructure → reach people → create creators → strengthen the city |
| Where does it happen? | Schools, libraries, public spaces — everywhere in Frankston |
| Who does it serve? | Children, families, artists — everyone |
| What does it produce? | New creators who stay and contribute |
| Does it end? | No — the return arrow shows it feeds itself |
| What's Council's role? | The top node. Council is the investment that starts the cycle. |
