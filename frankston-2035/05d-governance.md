# Governance
## Frankston 2035 — Decision Framework

---

### Governing Principle

Decisions are made at the lowest appropriate level.
The Constitution governs everything.
Everything else governs something specific.

---

### Decision Matrix

| Decision Type | Who Decides | Who Is Consulted | Who Is Informed |
|---|---|---|---|
| Creative direction (artworks, design) | Creative Director (BSS) | Relevant artist, school, or partner | Council liaison |
| Budget allocation within pilot | Creative Director (BSS) | BSS accounts | Council (quarterly report) |
| Artist selection | Creative Director (BSS) | Community, school, or library partner | Council liaison |
| Technology approval | Creative Director (BSS) against Charter | Healthy Technology Charter | Partner organisation |
| New partnership | Creative Director (BSS) | Community or institutional partner | All partners |
| Public statements about First Nations | Never unilaterally | First Nations advisors | All partners |
| Constitution amendments | Creative Director + Council | All partners | Community |
| Project scope changes | Creative Director + Council | All partners | Community |

---

### Advisory Structure

**Project partners (non-hierarchical):**
- Frankston City Council
- Frankston Library Service
- School network representatives
- Community representatives (to be identified per pilot)

**Senior advisors (as engaged):**
- First Nations cultural advisors — on their terms, not on demand
- Arts industry advisors
- Technology safety advisors

Advisory roles are consultative. They do not override the Creative Director or Council partnership agreement.

---

### Reporting

| Report | Frequency | Audience | Format |
|---|---|---|---|
| Progress update | Monthly | Council liaison | 1-page summary |
| Financial summary | Quarterly | Council, funders | Standard grant report |
| Milestone report | Per pilot phase | All partners | Full documentation |
| Public update | Bi-annual | Community | Plain language summary |

---

### Conflict Resolution

**Step 1:** Direct conversation between parties.
**Step 2:** Creative Director mediates.
**Step 3:** Written statement of positions. Both perspectives documented.
**Step 4:** If unresolved after 30 days, formal mediation.

No decision is made under duress. If a partner withdraws, the project continues with documentation of why.

---

### What Governance Is Not

- A committee that approves every creative decision
- A veto structure for individual Council members
- A mechanism for any single party to control the narrative
- A bureaucracy that slows down the work
