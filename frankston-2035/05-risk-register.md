# Risk Register
## Frankston 2035 — Version 1.0

---

### How to Read This Register

**Likelihood:** L = Low, M = Medium, H = High
**Impact:** L = Low, M = Medium, H = High
**Priority:** Likelihood × Impact (LL=1, LM=2, LH=3, ML=2, MM=4, MH=6, HL=3, HM=6, HH=9)

Register is reviewed at each project milestone and updated as conditions change.
