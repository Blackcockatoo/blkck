Privacy Policy

blkck2.com · Blue $nake Studio · The Moss Man

Last updated: June 2026

Short version: We do not collect your data. No ads. No trackers. No login. No analytics. No forms. The site does not send any information about you to us or to anyone else.

Who we are

Blue $nake Studio is an independent creative practice based in Frankston, Victoria, Australia, operated by The Moss Man (Tom Moss). Our creative portal lives at blkck2.com.

Contact: bluesssnakestudio@gmail.com or blkck2@gmail.com

What data we collect

None. This site does not collect personal information of any kind. There are no sign-up forms, no email capture, no account system, no tracking pixels, and no analytics scripts.

Cookies

We do not set any cookies.

Browser localStorage

The Strongman Tracker page (strongman-tracker.html) uses your browser's localStorage to remember which checklist items you have ticked. This data is stored entirely on your own device and is never transmitted anywhere. It can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

No other page on this site uses cookies, localStorage, or any form of client-side storage beyond what your browser does by default (e.g. standard HTTP caching).

Third-party services

Some pages on this site embed YouTube videos (for example, music content on the Black Wing Crew page). YouTube is operated by Google LLC and has its own privacy policy. When you play an embedded video, YouTube may set cookies or collect data according to their policies.

No other third-party scripts, embeds, social widgets, or advertising networks are used on this site.

Children

This site is designed to be safe for all audiences including children. We do not knowingly collect any data from children or adults. No names, photos, school details, voice recordings, or location data are collected by this site under any circumstances.

Some pages (Meta-Pet, Teacher Tools) are specifically designed for educational use by children and follow a strict no-data-collection, no-tracking, no-account approach.

Service workers and offline caching

This site uses a service worker to cache pages for offline use (a standard PWA feature). The cache stores copies of site files on your device only, so the site works without a network connection. No personal data is involved in this caching.

Your rights

Because we collect no personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, export, or delete. If you have any questions, contact us at bluesssnakestudio@gmail.com.

Changes to this policy

If anything changes — for example if we ever introduce a paid product with a different data model — this page will be updated and the "Last updated" date will change. The core principle will not change: no unnecessary data collection, no tracking, no ads.
