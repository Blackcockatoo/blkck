Short description

A creative portal for Blue $nake Studio: printables, music worlds, learning tools, gallery rooms and QR artefacts by The Moss Man.

Full description

Blue $nake Studio — Moss Man Portal is the official creative field device for blkck2.com.


Explore printable artefacts, music-world pages, child-safe learning tools, gallery rooms, symbolic systems, QR portals and proof-of-work links from The Moss Man / Blue $nake Studio in Frankston, Australia.


The app is designed as a simple, privacy-first creative portal:

  • no ads
  • no trackers
  • no login required
  • no social feed
  • no child data collection
  • no unnecessary permissions

Use it to open the B$S Print Vault, discover Black Wing Crew / Neon Venom, explore Meta-Pet and Teacher Tools, view gallery rooms, follow QR drops and contact the studio for workshops, school sessions, disability arts projects or custom creative work.

App store decision · release order

The pipeline.

Do these in order. Do not skip ahead.

  1. PWA first — works in any browser, installable, offline-capable
  2. Google Play internal test second — small, private, easy to iterate
  3. Google Play closed test third — 12 testers, 14 days minimum
  4. Public Play Store fourth — after proof and polish
  5. Apple App Store later — after Play Store is stable
