Who

The Moss Man / Tom Moss.

Tom Moss is a Frankston-based creator working across visual art, music, digital product development and symbolic systems. He creates under the name The Moss Man and produces under the studio entity Blue $nake Studio.

His work spans a connected set of disciplines that don't ordinarily appear together: painted mythology, behaviour-support tools for classrooms, digital music with street print assets, a visual number system called Moss 60 and a doctrine on language and meaning called Semantic Sovereignty.

The thread connecting all of it: patterns become worlds. The black cockatoo feather, the coiled snake, the base-60 geometry — these are the physical tokens of a single invented universe expressed across every medium the studio touches.

Tom has spent years building connected systems where a painting informs a number, a chant informs a classroom tool, and a recurring symbol threads through visual art, music, education technology and symbolic logic simultaneously.

What

What Blue $nake Studio makes.

Blue $nake Studio is The Moss Man's production entity. It operates across four disciplines simultaneously:

Education technology: Meta-Pet (privacy-first digital learning companion, no ads, no trackers, in development) and Teacher's Secret Cheatsheet (free printable behaviour-support templates for classrooms).

Music: Black Wing Crew, the Neon Venom 2026 LP, visual music releases with QR drops, lyric posters and street print assets.

Visual art: Ten gallery rooms including painted mythology, character portraits, humour and parody, colouring pages and print packs.

Symbolic systems: Moss 60 (a 60-position visual number system), the Oracle Warden (interactive glyph engine) and Semantic Sovereignty (a doctrine on language, framing and narrative defence).
