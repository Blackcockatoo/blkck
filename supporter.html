What this funds.

Blue $nake Studio is one person in Frankston — The Moss Man. Any purchase goes directly toward music production, maintaining the free school tools, printing costs, and making it possible to run workshops without burning out.

The portal stays free regardless. These packs and commissions exist because some people want more, and because more needs to get made.
