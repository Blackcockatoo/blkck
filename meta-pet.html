01 · What it is

A digital companion that gives attention back.

Meta-Pet is a concept in active development by The Moss Man / Blue $nake Studio. It combines a digital pet care model with STEAM learning mechanics, pattern recognition tasks and a symbolic identity system powered by Moss 60 base-60 geometry.

The core idea: a digital companion that children can care for, customise and learn with — without any of the design patterns that extract attention, money or data from children or families.

The prototype is available at bluesnakestudios.com. It runs in the browser — no download, no app store, no accounts. Open and use immediately. School pilot enquiries are welcome — contact the studio directly.

02 · Why it is safe

What Meta-Pet will never do.

🚫

No ads

No advertising. No sponsored content. No branded characters. No revenue model built on attention capture.

🔒

No trackers

No analytics tracking, no fingerprinting, no third-party data sharing. What happens in the app stays in the app.

📵

No social feed

No infinite scroll. No likes, shares or follower counts. No comparison or social pressure mechanics.

🎰

No gambling loops

No loot boxes. No random rewards engineered for compulsion. No pay-to-win or premium unlocks targeting children.

☁️

No unnecessary cloud data

Local-first design. Child data is not uploaded, profiled or sold. No accounts required for core features.

⏱️

Healthy session design

Designed to end. Sessions are bounded. No dark patterns to extend screen time beyond what the child or teacher intended.
